## What It Does

- **Car Listings:** Browse available cars with photos, specifications, and pricing
- **Search & Filter:** Find cars by make, model, year, price range, and other criteria
- **Car Details:** View comprehensive information about each vehicle
- **Inquiry System:** Submit inquiries to express interest in purchasing
- **Admin Dashboard:** Manage car inventory, add/edit/delete listings
- **Success Stories:** Showcase customer testimonials and successful purchases
- **Responsive Design:** Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
